Newb Shader

An aesthetic shader for Minecraft BE

Download

Make your gameplay more aesthetic!

Newb Shader focuses on improving the vanilla shader to look more aesthetic without ruining the gameplay or sacrificing much performance.

Screenshots

Features

Lighting: Overworld, underwater, nether, and end lighting.

Wave effects: Green plants/leaves wave, farm plants wave, lantern wave, and water wave.

Rain effects: Wet ground with sky reflection, mist blow.

Water: Simple sky reflection, smoother water texture.

Sky: Three color gradient.

Clouds: Soft clouds with rain transition and aurora.

Entities: Soft highlight around edges of entities.
