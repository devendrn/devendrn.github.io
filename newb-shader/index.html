Newb Shader 1.1.9

An aesthetic lightweight shader for Minecraft PE

Make your gameplay more aesthetic

Newb Shader focuses on improving the existing vanilla shader to look more aesthetic and realistic without ruining the existing gameplay or sacrificing much performance.


Features

Better rain, Natural lighting, 3D Moon, Auroras, Swinging Lantern, Foliage wave, Underwater effects, Glowy ores, New Sky, Better water

Most of the features are implemented in the vertex shader for better performance.
