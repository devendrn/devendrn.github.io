FAQs

Why is the shader invisible on my device?

It's probably a Minecraft import issue. Try to install the shader using other methods.


Why is the sky box black? or Why is everything vanilla?

The shader doesn't support your device. It only supports Android and iOS.


Why are the leaves/plants/grass color weird on my device?

It's a device specific bug. Use the lite version for now. The issue only happens on the main version.
