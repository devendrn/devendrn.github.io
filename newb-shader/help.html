FAQs

Frequently asked questions

Will the shader be updated?

Newb Shader is discontinued. There will be no new updates.

Why is the sky box black? or Why is everything vanilla?

The shader only works on Android/iOS running Minecraft 1.18.12 or less.

Why is the shader invisible?

It's probably a Minecraft import issue. Try to install the shader using other methods.

Why do the leaves/plants/grass color look weird?

It's a device specific bug. It has been fixed in the development release.

How can I customize the shader?

See Shader customization.

Can I modify or use the codes of the shader?

Yes.
